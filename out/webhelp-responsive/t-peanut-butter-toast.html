Peanut Butter Toast and Fruit

This recipe is for a quick and easy breakfast of toasted bread with delicious fruit.

You will need the following ingredients:
  • 1 slice of whole grain bread (gluten-free if needed)
  • ½ tablespoons of peanut butter
  • fruit toppings of choice: banana slices, strawberries, sliced apples, etc.
This recipe is adapted from Eating Bird Food's Peanut Butter Breakfast Toast article.
  1. Toast the whole grain bread
  2. Spread on the peanut butter in even strokes
  3. Sprinkle on your choice of fruit toppings—For non-vegans, consider drizzling honey over the peanut butter toast for a burst of flavor
