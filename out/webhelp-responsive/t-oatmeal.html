Oh, My Oatmeal!

This recipe is for oatmeal, which is a hot breakfast made from oats.

You will need the following ingredients:
  • 1 tablespoon of unsalted butter
  • 1 large pinch kosher salt
  • 1 ½ cups of water
  • 1 cup of quick-cooking oats
  • 1 orange or lemon
  • 1 handful fresh or dried fruit
  • brown sugar
  • milk
  • 1 handful of nuts
This recipe is adapted from the Food Network cookbook How to Boil Water: Life Beyond Takeout.
  1. Combine the butter, salt, and water in a small saucepan and bring to a boil
  2. Finely grate the citrus zest into the pan
  3. Bring to a boil again, reduce to a simmer, and stir in fruit
  4. Stir constantly until oats are cooked and dried fruit is slightly plumped for 2 minutes
  5. Divide into 2 bowls and sprinkle with sweetener of your choice
  6. Drizzle with non-dairy milk and top off with nuts
